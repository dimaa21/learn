Adidas Originals OZELIA

You may be far from the 90s, but the Ozelia came from that era of colorful experimentation. Inspired by archival adidas models, these chunky sneakers are designed to be eye-catching. The dynamic design and soft, comfortable Adiprene cushioning outsole make them perfect for active days. Adidas OZWEEGO

These sneakers represent the style of the late 90s and early 2000s in a new way, combining retro details with a futuristic design. The model is made of mesh, suede and smooth synthetic material. Special inserts support the forefoot and heel. The sneakers feel incredibly comfortable all day long. Vintage details from the signature design of the 1998 model. Ozweego borrowed the transparent fixing cords, the midsole with a recognizable shape and the iconic lacing bar. The Adiprene+ midsole provides a cushioned stride and increased repulsive force.
